About Me

Hello World! My name is Sam G. Zun and I'd like to welcome you to my personal website! For the past few years I have been self-teaching myself coding through resources such as Codecademy and Free Code Camp, exploring HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, Ruby and Ruby on Rails.
